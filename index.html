WHY CHOOSE US

Our Strength

“Mubuga Bio Coffee is by its name organic, thus it is set to maximize the organic coffee production systems. The Company will produce its own manure using coffee pulp. As the local population will be organized in farming cooperatives, the manure produced from their livestock will be used in coffee plantation to supplement that produced by the Company, hence achieving a total reliance on organic manure, which has no adverse effect on the environment. The company will also join the national efforts of planting trees, creating orchard/radical terraces to protect the environment. It is worth noting that coffee trees, having deep roots contribute to soil reinforcement, hence serving both purposes- commercial and soil protection.”

“Mubuga Bio-Coffee is a company that is investing in Bio-Coffee to improve the livelihood of the local population while uplifting the Sector of Mubuga, commonly known by the negative effect of the genocide against the Tutsi in 1994….”
